Karthik Vaidhyanathan moderated the opening panel discussion on **"Net-Zero Software: Rethinking Tech’s Role in Climate Action"** at the annual R&D Showcase of IIIT Hyderabad. The panel, which included SA4S member **Srinivasan Rakhunathan**, addressed critical topics in the field of sustainable computing, including the definition of Green Software, the development of standard sustainability metrics, and the industrial challenges of adopting Green AI.

In addition to the panel, Karthik Vaidhyanathan moderated a roundtable session titled **"AI for Greener Tomorrow,"** facilitating discussions on the intersection of artificial intelligence and environmental responsibility.

### Research and Product Showcase

The SA4S research group was well-represented throughout the event, with students presenting their ongoing research at the Software Engineering Research Center (SERC). Furthermore, two innovative works from the group were selected for the CIE IIIT Hyderabad product showcase:

*   **ArchCoPilot**: An architectural assistant tool.
*   **LoCoML**: A framework for low-carbon machine learning.

These presentations highlight the group's ongoing efforts to translate research in software architecture and sustainability into practical tools for the broader technology ecosystem.
