**Karthik Vaidhyanathan** was a guest on the **#EnvironmentVariables podcast** by the **Green Software Foundation**, hosted by Anne Currie. In this engaging session, Karthik shared insights on **software sustainability**, with a special focus on **sustainable AI-enabled systems** and **#greenAI** 🌱🤖.

<img src='/images/pic/green-software-podcast.jpeg' class='img-responsive' alt='Karthik Vaidhyanathan on the Green Software Foundation podcast'/>  
Karthik discussing sustainable AI practices with Anne Currie on the Green Software Foundation podcast.  

The conversation highlighted key initiatives from the **SA4S group** at IIIT Hyderabad’s Software Engineering Research Center, including **dynamic model switching**, **sustainable MLOps**, and **edgeML Balancer**. Particular emphasis was given to Karthik’s **ANRF-Prime Minister Early Career Research Grant (PMECRG) project, #SustAInd**, which aims to develop a software-centric framework for building **sustainable generative and agentic AI systems**.

Karthik also acknowledged influential mentors, including **Patricia Lago**, for inspiring his work in software sustainability, and **Henry Muccini**, for his ongoing collaboration in AI-enabled software architecture research.
