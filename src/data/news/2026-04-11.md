The **SA4S research group** at the Software Engineering Research Center (SERC), IIIT Hyderabad, demonstrated a significant presence at the **International Conference on Software Engineering (ICSE) 2026**. The group presented nine separate works across the main conference and its co-located venues, focusing on the core themes of **Software Architecture**, **Agentic AI**, and **Sustainability**.

### 🔬 Research Contributions

The group's contributions highlighted how Generative AI and Agentic AI are reshaping the software development life cycle (SDLC) from design to operations. Key research works included:

*   **Main Track:** A research paper investigating the energy efficiency of code generated by Large Language Models (LLMs).
*   **SEAMS (Symposium on the Software Engineering for Adaptive and Self-Managing Systems):** Three papers (full, short, and artifact) focusing on leveraging agentic AI for self-adaptation, self-adaptive model routing, and sustainable MLOps.
*   **AGENT Workshop:** Three papers exploring agentic AI for architecture knowledge management, the energy efficiency of agentic code generation, and the evaluation of agentic AI systems.
*   **CAIN (Conference on AI Engineering):** A short paper on architecting AgentOps.
*   **Birds of a Feather (BoF):** A session focused on architecting self-coding information systems.

### 🏛️ Leadership and Participation

**Karthik Vaidhyanathan** took on several leadership roles during the conference, including co-chairing **GREENS 2026** and the **CAIN Industry Track**, as well as chairing a session at the main ICSE conference. Additionally, he attended the IEEE Software editorial board meeting and the ICSE 2027 Organizing Committee meeting.

SA4S student **Sreemaee Akshathala** represented the group as an ICSE Student Volunteer and presented research at the **AGENT 2026** workshop.

### 🚀 Research Direction

The extensive participation at ICSE 2026 reflects the SA4S group's commitment to three growing research pillars:
1.  **Engineering AI Systems (SE4AI):** Developing principled methods for building AI-driven software.
2.  **Using AI for Software Engineering (AI4SE):** Leveraging AI to improve software engineering processes.
3.  **Sustainability:** Establishing energy efficiency and sustainability as first-class architectural concerns.
