[TITLE] ArchBench: Benchmarking LLMs on Software Architecture Tasks
[TAG] Research
[CONTENT] ArchBench is the first unified benchmark platform for evaluating LLM capabilities on software architecture tasks — aggregating datasets from five research tasks including ADR generation, serverless component generation, and traceability link recovery under a standardised CLI evaluation pipeline and public leaderboard. The plugin architecture lets the community contribute new tasks and submit results without modifying the core framework, addressing a longstanding gap where the absence of shared evaluation standards made it impossible to compare or track LLM progress on architectural tasks.
