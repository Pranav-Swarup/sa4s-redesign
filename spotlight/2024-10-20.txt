[TITLE] Best Poster at ICSA 2024
[TAG] Award
[PREVIEW] Software Architecture Conference, Prague
[CONTENT] SA4S won the Best Poster Award at the International Conference on Software Architecture 2024 in Prague, Czech Republic — one of the top venues for software architecture research worldwide. The awarded poster presented our work on self-adaptive ML systems, demonstrating how architectural patterns can be applied to make machine learning pipelines more robust and energy-efficient in production.
